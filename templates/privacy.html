Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Expendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Expendly:

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your account.
  • To display your expense history, summaries, and category breakdowns.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and access controls.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Expendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use limited third-party infrastructure (e.g., hosting providers) strictly to operate the Service. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, analytics brokers, or data marketplaces.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the contact information provided on our site.
